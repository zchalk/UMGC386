Many users primarily utlize their phones or tablets rather than computers for internet browsing, repsonsive design ensures users on any device are able to easily and clearly interact with a site.

Google Analytics

Three google analytic reports that help developers understand the information users are accessing are:

  • Behavior Flow Report

    • The Behavioral Flow Report gives a visual guide of how users are navigating through a given website. It shows typical user flow from one area to the next. It also shows where users typically enter and exit a site. For example they might always open at the login screen rather than the home page.

  • Site Content Report

    • The Top Content section of the Site Content Report shows what pages are most visited for any given site. This allows developers to easily identify what pages recieve the most attention. This can help you expand what works and decrease what doesn't.

  • Site Search Report

    • The Site Search Report shows what the most popular search terms are on any given website. It also shows what pages users visit with those specfic search terms. Simlar to trending topics and tweets on X.
